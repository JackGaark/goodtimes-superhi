About

Ansa is a 2011 Recipient of the Minnesota Playwright Center's McKnight Fellowship. City Pages named him Best Actor in 2007. He has appeared at Mixed Blood Theatre, History Theatre, Black Ensemble Theatre, Chicago Theatre Company, Frank Theatre, and many other stages around the country.
"Acting is as much an act of spacing as an act of marking."
